## LinkedIn Copy

I turned one of my strangest AI experiments into a small public artwork.

`honeymoon` began as Hinge automation: a Jeffrey/OpenClaw agent operating on a
real iPhone, reading profiles through accessibility snapshots, writing comments,
remembering my taste, and avoiding unsafe or paid actions.

The technical experiment worked. The human part got complicated.

So I made a terminal film about the thing no automation system can solve: the
heart does not have a stable API.

This is not a dating bot launch. It is a story about what happens when agents
move from productivity into intimacy.
